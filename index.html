Display Technology

Understanding display defects is crucial for hardware maintenance. "Dead Pixels" are black spots resulting from power failure to a pixel, while "Stuck Pixels" are frozen in Red, Green, or Blue. Our tool uses high-contrast cycling to help identify and potentially revive stuck liquid crystals.

Regular testing prevents eye strain caused by backlight bleeding and ensures your monitor operates at peak performance.

Input & Reflex Specs

N-Key Rollover (NKRO) is essential for gaming. It ensures every keystroke is registered even when multiple keys are pressed simultaneously. Ghosting occurs when signal conflicts cause unregistered inputs.
